PbNotify: The Pebble Notification API


create new notification

/notification/create/

https://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/create/<userid>/<source>/<text>
userid: your generated userid
source: the originating entity of your notification
text: the text of your notification

returns: json-encoded notificationid

$ curl "http://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/create/2150a001dab1d068d13ee5f5/Test/HelloWorld"
{"1": "notificationid", "2": "527ded52049fed00073e9c0f"}

retrieve most recent notification

/notification/get/

https://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/get/<userid>
userid: your generated userid

returns: pebble-friendly json-encoded notification (source and text)

$ curl "http://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/get/2150a001dab1d068d13ee5f5"
{"1": "source", "2": "Test", "3": "text", "4": "HelloWorld"}

retrieve notification

/notification/get/

https://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/get/<userid>/<notificationid>
userid: your generated userid
notificationid: the notificationid returned from a previous `/notification/create/` call

returns: pebble-friendly json-encoded notification (source and text)

$ curl "http://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/get/2150a001dab1d068d13ee5f5/527ded52049fed00073e9c0f"
{"1": "source", "2": "Test", "3": "text", "4": "HelloWorld"}

mark most recent notification as delivered

/notification/delivered/

https://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/delivered/<userid>
userid: your generated userid

returns: json-encoded notificationid

$ curl "http://pbnotify.herokuapp.com/api/notification/delivered/2150a001dab1d068d13ee5f5"
{"1": "notificationid", "2": "527ded52049fed00073e9c0f"}
